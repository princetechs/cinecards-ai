# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in CineCards AI respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections gracefully.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Credit sources, collaborators, and rights holders.
- Respect privacy and consent around contributed media.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Submitting media without the right to use it.
- Repeated disruption of issues, discussions, or reviews.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Reports can be sent privately to the maintainers listed in the repository.

This code of conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant.
